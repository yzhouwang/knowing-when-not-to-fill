Through friendly consultations, the parties have entered into the following agreement:

## II. Capital Contribution and Interest Ratio

The amount, form and ratio of capital contribution of Party A and Party B are as follows:

**Party A ({{partyAName}}) shall contribute:**

1. Proprietary AI technology platform, software, and related intellectual property (licensed, not transferred);
2. Technical personnel for deployment, training, and ongoing support;
3. Capital contribution: {{partyACapitalAmount}};
4. International marketing and brand support.

**Party B shall contribute:**

1. Local market intelligence, client introductions, and government/regulatory relationships;
2. In-country operational infrastructure (office space, local staff, logistics);
3. Regulatory compliance support, including obtaining necessary licenses, permits, and approvals;
4. Capital contribution: {{partyBCapitalAmount}};
5. Ongoing local client relationship management and support.

## III. Business Purpose and Strategic Positioning

The Parties confirm that the core purpose of establishing the Partnership is to build a strategic platform in {{targetCountry}}:

1. Market Development: To conduct market research, brand promotion, and channel development in {{targetCountry}} by leveraging the resources of both Parties;
2. Project Coordination: To provide business opportunity referrals and assist clients in communication and negotiation with local governments or enterprises;
3. Preliminary Coordination: To serve as a window for the Parties' cooperation in {{targetCountry}}, coordinating various matters in the preliminary stages of projects.

## IV. Profit Distribution

**Distribution Principle:** The Partnership shall distribute its distributable profits after taxation for the current year according to the proportion of capital contributions subscribed by both parties.

**Timing of Distribution:** Profit distribution shall be carried out within {{profitDistributionDays}} days after the submission of the annual audit report, the convening of the partners' meeting, and the adoption of the dividend resolution.

**Reserve Fund:** Prior to any profit distribution, the Partnership must allocate statutory reserves in accordance with the law of {{targetCountry}}.

**Dedicated Account:** The Partnership shall maintain a dedicated bank account in {{targetCountry}}. All Project revenue shall be deposited into and all Project expenses paid from this account.

**Financial Reporting:** Party B (or the appointed manager) shall prepare and deliver to Party A monthly financial statements and a quarterly management report within {{financialReportingDays}} days after the end of each quarter.

**Audit:** The Partnership's accounts shall be audited annually by an independent auditor mutually agreed upon by the Parties. Each Party shall have the right to inspect the books and records at any reasonable time.

## V. Governance Structure

**Partners' Meeting:** The Partners' Meeting, composed of all partners, shall be the supreme governing body of the Partnership. Regular meetings shall be held (at least every {{meetingFrequencyMonths}} months) to review strategic direction, budgets, and major decisions.

**Day-to-Day Management:** Day-to-day operations of the {{targetCountry}} Project shall be managed by Party B or a jointly appointed Project Manager, subject to the Partners' Meeting oversight.

**Reserved Matters:** The following decisions shall require the unanimous written consent of both Parties:

1. Annual budget approval and any expenditure exceeding {{expenditureThresholdUSD}} USD;
2. Entering into any contract with a value exceeding {{contractThresholdUSD}} USD;
3. Hiring or terminating senior management;
4. Admitting a new partner or transferring any partnership interest;
5. Any litigation, arbitration, or settlement exceeding {{settlementThresholdUSD}} USD;
6. Amendment of this Agreement;
7. Dissolution of the Partnership.

## VI. Intellectual Property

**Background IP:** Each Party retains full ownership of its pre-existing intellectual property ("Background IP"). Party A's AI platform, algorithms, models, and related technology remain the sole property of Party A.

**License:** Party A grants the Partnership a non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable license to use Party A's technology solely for the purpose of the {{targetCountry}} Project during the Term.

**Foreground IP:** Any new intellectual property created jointly in the course of the Project ("Foreground IP") shall be jointly owned by the Parties in proportion to their partnership interests, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

**Upon Termination:** Upon termination of this Agreement, the license granted above shall immediately cease, and Party B shall return or destroy all copies of Party A's proprietary technology and materials.

## VII. Confidentiality

Each Party shall keep confidential all non-public information received from the other Party ("Confidential Information"), including business plans, financial data, client information, technical specifications, and the terms of this Agreement.

Confidential Information shall not be disclosed to third parties without the prior written consent of the disclosing Party, except as required by law, regulation, or stock exchange rules.

This obligation shall survive termination for {{confidentialityTermYears}} years.

## VIII. Anti-Corruption and Compliance

Each Party shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations in its respective jurisdiction, including data protection laws, tax laws, and business licensing requirements.

Each Party represents and warrants that it shall comply with all applicable anti-bribery and anti-corruption laws, including the U.S. Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA) and the UK Bribery Act 2010.

Neither Party shall, directly or indirectly, make any payment, gift, or offer of value to any government official, political party, or public entity in connection with the Project.

Each Party shall maintain accurate books and records and shall cooperate with any audit or investigation related to anti-corruption compliance.

## IX. Data Protection

The Parties shall comply with {{dataProtectionLaw}} and any other applicable data protection and privacy laws.

Where personal data is processed in connection with the Project, the Parties shall enter into a separate data processing agreement setting out roles, responsibilities, and technical and organizational measures.

## X. Liability for Breach

Each Party shall be liable for losses caused by its own negligence, willful misconduct, or breach of this Agreement.

Each Party shall indemnify and hold harmless the other Party from and against any claims, damages, or liabilities arising from the indemnifying Party's breach of its representations, warranties, or obligations under this Agreement.

Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages except in cases of willful misconduct or fraud.

## XI. Force Majeure

Neither Party shall be liable for failure or delay in performance due to events beyond its reasonable control, including natural disasters, war, terrorism, pandemics, sanctions, government actions, or infrastructure failures, provided the affected Party gives prompt notice and uses reasonable efforts to mitigate the impact.
