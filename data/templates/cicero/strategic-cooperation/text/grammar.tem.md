## I. Cooperation Content and Objectives

**Article 1: Cooperation Areas**

The Parties shall cooperate to mutually support product sales, business development, and data center-related engineering services. Party B shall provide Party A with business opportunity recommendations, assistance in communication and negotiation, and perform preliminary project coordination.

**Article 2: Rights and Obligations**

**Rights and obligations of Party A:** Party A shall provide the necessary documentation related to the project and shall ensure the authenticity, accuracy and completeness of the materials provided. Party A shall be responsible for the quality of the products and services it provides, ensuring that they comply with the technical standards agreed upon by both parties, industry specifications, and the requirements of applicable laws and regulations.

**Rights and obligations of Party B:** Upon written authorization by Party A, Party B may conduct business negotiations and liaisons on behalf of Party A. Pursuant to this Agreement, Party B has the right to obtain all necessary documents, materials, and technical support required for this purpose. Party B shall take the lead in managing the customers and cooperative projects it secures, including, but not limited to, evaluation, technical discussions, and commercial negotiations. Within the scope of this strategic cooperation, Party B is granted business development authority commensurate with that of Party A. In instances where Party B and any agent, partner, or channel designated by Party A engage with the same potential customer concurrently, the customer relationship shall be attributed to the party that first reaches a cooperation intention agreement with said customer.

Both Parties shall exercise due diligence and professional care, performing their respective cooperation duties in good faith. During the term of the cooperation, both Parties shall actively safeguard and enhance the reputation and image of the other Party, its products, services, and brand, and shall not, by any act or omission, impair the legitimate interests, business opportunities, or market reputation of the other Party. Each Party shall be independently responsible for its own regulatory compliance and lawful operations; compliance matters of one Party shall not constitute an obligation or liability of the other Party.

**Article 3: Benefit Distribution**

As strategic partners, both Parties shall be entitled to benefit from the performance of the Strategic Cooperation Agreement. With respect to the products promoted and the sales revenue generated therefrom by Party B, Party B shall possess profit distribution rights equal to those of Party A. The specific profit-sharing terms, which may vary based on product type and project type, shall be separately negotiated and detailed by the Parties.

## II. Confidentiality Clause

Both Parties shall keep confidential all business secrets and technical information for {{confidentialityTermYears}} years after termination, as follows:

**Confidential Information:** Non-public information disclosed by one Party in any form (including, but not limited to, business, technical, financial, pricing, customer, contractual information, and know-how).

**Obligations:** The Receiving Party shall use the Confidential Information solely for the purpose of performing this Agreement; may disclose it only to those of its representatives who have a need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations no less protective than those herein; and shall employ protective measures no less rigorous than a reasonable degree of care.

**Exceptions:** This obligation shall not apply to information that is publicly available, lawfully obtained from a third party without confidentiality restrictions, independently developed without reference to the Confidential Information, or disclosed with prior written consent.

**Compelled Disclosure:** If either Party is required to disclose any Confidential Information pursuant to applicable laws or regulatory requirements, it shall provide the Disclosing Party with prior notice to the extent reasonably practicable, and the disclosure shall be limited to the minimum extent necessary.

**Term:** The confidentiality obligations hereunder shall survive the termination of this Agreement for a period of {{confidentialityTermYears}} years; with respect to trade secrets, such protection shall continue during their existence.

**Return/Destruction:** Upon termination or upon request, the Receiving Party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information, except that it may retain one copy solely for compliance or archival purposes, which shall remain subject to the confidentiality obligations herein.
